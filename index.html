How JobHunter Helps

Tools designed with your job search journey in mind.

Find the Right Opportunities

Stop jumping between different job sites. JobHunter brings everything together.

  • Search across LinkedIn, job boards, and company sites
  • Discover hidden jobs in company docs and Notion pages
  • Use AI to generate effective search queries
  • Filter by experience level and location

Create Tailored Applications

Stand out with personalized CVs and cover letters for each position you apply to.

  • AI-powered CV customization for each job
  • Automatic cover letter generation
  • ATS optimization to get past filtering systems
  • Interview preparation assistance

Track Progress & Develop Skills

Keep everything organized and learn what skills can help advance your career.

  • Track all your applications in one place
  • Identify skill gaps based on jobs you're interested in
  • Get personalized learning recommendations
  • Monitor your career development progress

Choose Your Experience

Two ways to use JobHunter, depending on your preference.

Web App

Access JobHunter from any browser, on any device. Perfect if you prefer a simple, accessible experience.

  • No installation required
  • Works on any device with a browser
  • Manual job entry and organization

Browser Extension

Add JobHunter to your Firefox browser for enhanced functionality and a more integrated experience.

  • Auto-extract job details from listings

Our Vision

We're building JobHunter to solve real problems in the job search process. By offering it for free while we develop, we can learn directly from job seekers like you about what works and what doesn't.

Your feedback helps us create something truly valuable - and many features will always remain free, even as we refine our approach.

We believe that finding meaningful work shouldn't be an overwhelming experience, and we're committed to making that vision a reality.
